üìä RFM Methodology

RFM Analysis is a customer segmentation technique that uses three key metrics:

  • Recency: How recently did the customer make a purchase?
  • Frequency: How often do they purchase?
  • Monetary: How much do they spend?

10 Customer Segments Identified

1 Champions: Best customers - bought recently, buy often, and spend the most
2 Loyal Customers: Spend good money and buy regularly
3 Potential Loyalists: Recent customers with average frequency and spending
4 Recent Customers: Bought most recently but not frequently
5 Promising: Recent shoppers with decent spending
6 Customers Needing Attention: Above average recency, frequency, and monetary values
7 About to Sleep: Below average recency and frequency
8 At Risk: Spent big money and purchased often, but long time ago
9 Can't Lose Them: Made big purchases and often, but haven't returned in a while
10 Hibernating: Last purchase was long ago, low spenders, and low frequency

üí° Business Impact & Strategies

Targeted Marketing Strategies by Segment

  • Champions & Loyal: VIP programs, early access to new products, exclusive deals
  • Potential Loyalists: Loyalty programs, membership benefits, personalized recommendations
  • At Risk: Win-back campaigns, special discounts, "We miss you" communications
  • Recent Customers: Onboarding programs, product education, engagement campaigns
  • Hibernating: Aggressive discount campaigns, product reminders, reactivation offers

Expected Business Outcomes

  • Increase customer retention by 25-30% through targeted campaigns
  • Boost average order value (AOV) by focusing on high-potential segments
  • Reduce marketing spend waste by avoiding non-responsive segments
  • Improve customer lifetime value (CLV) through segment-specific strategies
  • Enable data-driven decision making for marketing budget allocation

üìà Key Insights

Segment Distribution Findings

  • Champions: Small but valuable - highest revenue per customer
  • At Risk: Significant revenue opportunity if successfully reactivated
  • Hibernating: Largest segment - potential for mass reactivation campaigns
  • Recent Customers: Focus on conversion to loyalty programs

Revenue Impact Analysis

  • Top 20% of customers (Champions + Loyal) generate 60% of revenue
  • Middle 30% (Potential Loyalists + Needing Attention) generate 25% of revenue
  • Bottom 50% (Hibernating + At Risk) generate 15% but have reactivation potential
